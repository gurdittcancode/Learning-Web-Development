                    BACKGROUND

1. Background image
    url(*write url*)

2. Background size
    cover, contain, auto
    - 'contain' scales the image as large as possible without 
    cropping or stretching

    - 'cover' scale the image as large as possible without
        stretching but it will crop it


3. Background repeat

4. Background position
    - where the background starts
        is it aligned to the top of the picture, bottom of the picture etc

5. BACKGROUND shorthand
    order doesn't matter in most cases

** BUT SIZE MUST IMMEDIATELY FOLLOW THE POSITION WITH '/'
    center/cover
    otherwise the image won't show

6. You can have multiple images. Comma-separated urls
